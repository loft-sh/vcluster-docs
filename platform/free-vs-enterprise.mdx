# Compare free and enterprise tiers

vCluster Platform is available in multiple tiers to serve different use cases, from individual developers to enterprise production deployments. This guide explains the differences between the Free and Enterprise tiers.

## Overview

**vCluster Platform Free tier** provides core platform capabilities for managing virtual clusters, users, and projects. The Free tier is available at no cost and requires only an account activation.

**vCluster Platform Enterprise tier** adds advanced features for production deployments, compliance requirements, and large-scale operations.

For detailed pricing information, see the [vCluster Pricing](https://www.vcluster.com/pricing) page.

:::info
The Free tier requires activating your Platform instance, but remains free to use with no credit card required.
:::

### What's included in Free tier

The Platform Free tier provides essential capabilities for managing virtual clusters:

- **Projects and RBAC**: Organize resources by team with role-based access control
- **Connected clusters**: Connect and manage multiple Kubernetes clusters
- **Cluster roles**: Define and assign roles across your clusters
- **Secrets management**: Securely store and distribute secrets
- **Apps and templates**: Deploy applications using templates and Helm charts
- **Namespaces**: Create and manage Kubernetes namespaces
- **Custom branding**: Customize the Platform UI with your organization's branding
- **OIDC provider**: Basic identity provider capabilities

### What's added in Enterprise tier

The Enterprise tier includes all Free tier features plus:

- **[Sleep mode](/platform/use-platform/virtual-clusters/sleep-mode)**: Automatic scaling based on activity to reduce costs
- **[High availability](/platform/configure/high-availability)**: Multi-replica deployment for production reliability
- **[SSO integration](/platform/configure/auth/sso)**: Enterprise authentication with SAML, LDAP, OAuth, GitHub, GitLab
- **[Audit logging](/platform/administer/audit-log)**: Comprehensive logging of all platform activities
- **[Air-gapped deployment](/platform/install/advanced/air-gapped)**: Deploy in disconnected environments
- **Multiple SSO providers**: Support for multiple identity providers simultaneously
- **Enterprise support**: Dedicated support and SLAs

## Activate the free tier

To activate the Platform Free tier:

1. **Deploy vCluster Platform**: Follow the [installation guide](/platform/install/) to deploy the Platform
2. **Activate your instance**: When you first access the Platform UI, you'll be prompted to activate with your email address
3. **Verify activation**: Check your email for the activation link and complete the process

No credit card is required for the Free tier.

## Upgrade to enterprise

To upgrade from Free to Enterprise tier:

1. Visit the [vCluster Pricing](https://www.vcluster.com/pricing) page
2. Contact the [sales team](https://www.vcluster.com/enterprise-demo) for a demo
3. Your existing Platform instance can be upgraded without redeployment
