To delete a project secret follow these steps:

<Flow id="secret-delete-project-secret-data">
  <Step>
    Go to <NavStep>Projects</NavStep>, select your project, then click <NavStep>Secrets</NavStep>.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click <Label>Delete</Label> in the drop-down menu next to your secret.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click <Label>Delete</Label> in the confirmation dialog.
  </Step>
</Flow>
